Data Wrangling. Making existing data more accessible.

One of the most time-consuming steps in any data analysis is cleaning the data and getting it into a format that allows analysis. Data wrangling is the process of converting and/or mapping data from one form into a format that allows for more convenient use in analytics. We organize disparate data sets collected by multiple parties, including conversion of PDF files to machine readable formats and data scraping.

Our project, Jersey City Budget PDF Conversion, won first place at the Liberation Event. 3,000+ pages of Jersey City budgets were converted from PDF into spreadsheets to be used by the public for analysis and discussion.

Civic Tech Labs. #opendata & #civictech advocacy.

We actively advocate for #opendata and support #civictech. Data Gallery founder, Anna Lukasiak, is one of the leading open data advocates in the state of New Jersey, USA.

In 2013 she started CodeForJC, a local Code For America Brigade, to help bridge the gap between the local government and residents through digital projects.

In May 2014, she co-organized the NJ Open Data Summit, the first New Jersey conference that brought journalists, public employees, and developers together to discuss open data.

In 2019, we launched a new project CertVerifyMe in collaboration Morrison Technologies to provide a platform for easy and secure verification of proffesional certificates.
